// CORPORATE INCENTIVES

While working for Corporate Incentives Dave's main responsibilities were the development of the One2OneMail product. One2OneMail was a Flash based application that allowed real estate agents to design their own post cards. Users can upload images, create custom text, colors for their postcards, upload mailing lists and actually produce mailable postcards.

The product was great in theory but was a bit ahead of it's time and never went anywhere (also due to the real estate market crashing).

Technologies Used

  • Flash
  • ActionScript 1.0 - 2.0
  • Classic ASP
  • JavaScript
  • VB Script
